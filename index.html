Message from Coordinator

Delhi Technological University (DTU), Delhi has emerged as one the premier Technological institutions of our great country. DTU provides with state of the art facilities, world class education, training, research and consultancy in the arena of Science, Engineering, Technology, and Management. DTU has been an epitome of quality education with a mission of developing exceptional quality technocrats. To encourage innovation, DTU has established Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Cell, that works closely under the Industrial R&D unit, to ensure the protection of claims of the Inventor(s) of DTU, i.e. faculty/students/supporting staff/project staff/visitors of DTU through the choice of intellectual property protection on a unique work, ideas, inventions, products, technology or to keep it open for public domain, whichever they find appropriate.

Dr Madan Mohan Tripathi
Coordinator, IPR Cell
Delhi Technological University
